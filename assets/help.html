Q: How do I activate a GalacticNight function with Tasker?
A: When setting a Tasker action, choose "App", then "Load", and then select GalacticNight. Then under "Data" put in one of the ScreenDim action codes, and put a checkmark beside "Exclude From Recent Apps". The following are the ScreenDim action codes:
1, 2, 3, 4 and 5: load preset 1-5
auto: disable GalacticNight and set automatic brightness
cycle: cycle through GalacticNight presets
